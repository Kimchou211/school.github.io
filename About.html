About MINYLA Computer School

Empowering the next generation of technology professionals since 2010

Our Story

MINYLA Computer School was founded in 2010 with a mission to make quality computer education accessible to everyone. We believe that in today's digital world, computer literacy is not just an advantage but a necessity.

What started as a small training center with just two instructors has now grown into a premier computer education institution with multiple locations and thousands of successful graduates. Our journey has been guided by our commitment to excellence and our passion for technology education.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with the technical skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the digital economy, regardless of their background or prior experience. We strive to make technology education accessible, practical, and relevant to real-world needs.

Our Vision

To be Cambodia's leading computer education provider, recognized for producing highly skilled technology professionals who drive innovation and digital transformation across all sectors of society.

Our Values

  • Quality Education: We're committed to providing the highest standard of instruction with up-to-date curriculum and teaching methodologies.
  • Accessibility: We believe everyone should have access to tech education, regardless of their background or financial situation.
  • Innovation: We continuously update our curriculum to reflect the latest industry trends and technological advancements.
  • Community: We foster a supportive learning environment where students can collaborate and grow together.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from course design to student support services.
